How to make the best Grilled Cheese

What you will need:

  • Pan
  • Stovetop

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread of your choice
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Shredded cheese of your choice

Getting started

  1. Add butter and mayonnaise to your bread
  2. Put the slices in a pan butter side up
  3. Flip after 30 seconds or when warm
  4. Add shredded cheese
  5. Put slices together cheese sides touching
  6. Cook until cheese melts or to your preference

Enjoy your sandwich!
